Nova UI - Cosmic Library

Theme Overview

Nova UI features four space-inspired themes, each designed to create a unique experience:

  • Supernova: Vibrant theme with explosive orange energy and electric blue accents, inspired by the dramatic stellar explosions.
  • Cosmic Blue: Cool blue theme reminiscent of hot blue stars and galaxies, with a serene and professional feel.
  • Nebula: Colorful and vibrant theme with pinks, purples, and blues inspired by the beautiful cosmic nebulae.
  • Dark Matter: Subtle and mysterious theme with a focus on light blue accents against a very dark background, representing the enigmatic dark matter.

All themes feature subtle glow effects to evoke the luminosity of cosmic phenomena and come with dark mode as standard.
